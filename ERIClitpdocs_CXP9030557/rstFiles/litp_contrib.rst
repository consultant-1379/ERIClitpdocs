==================================
 Contributed Plugins Information
==================================

The documents listed below provide detailed information on the Contributed LITP plugins, 
their property types and item types. Contributed LITP plugins are developed,
owned and maintained by teams external to the base LITP product team. For 
more information and for contact details on these plugins, please visit the 
`LITP 2 Contributed Plugins section of the Community Developed Software portal. <https://cds.ericsson.se/#portal/it-platform>`__ |external|

Note: these plugins are available separately and not as part of the LITP ISO.
